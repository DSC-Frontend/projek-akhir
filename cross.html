Cross (Creating the White Cross)

The first step in the CFOP method is to form a white cross on the bottom layer. While this step seems simple, solving the cross efficiently is crucial for reducing solving time.

Techniques for Solving the Cross

  • Beginners usually solve the cross one piece at a time, but speedcubing techniques emphasize forming the cross efficiently, considering the position of the edge pieces relative to the centers.
  • Ideally, this step can be completed in 4-8 moves.
  • A skilled cuber can solve the cross in under 2 seconds without excessive cube rotations.
