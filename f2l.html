F2L (First Two Layers)

After solving the cross, the next step is to complete the first two layers by solving four corner-edge pairs simultaneously.

F2L Solving Methods

    Beginners often solve corners first and then edges, but in CFOP, corner and edge pieces are paired simultaneously to reduce move count.Ideally, this step can be completed in 4-8 moves.
  • When performed efficiently, each pair can be solved in an average of 7-8 moves, meaning the entire F2L can be completed in 30-40 moves.
