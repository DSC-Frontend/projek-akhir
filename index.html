What is Cubiks?

Cubiks is a comprehensive guide website for solving the Rubik's Cube using the CFOP method (Cross, F2L, OLL, and PLL). We provide detailed step-by-step instructions, illustrations, and tips to help both beginners and speedcubers improve their skills. With Cubiks, learning to solve the Rubik's Cube becomes easier, more systematic, and faster.
