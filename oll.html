OLL (Orientation of the Last Layer)

Once the first two layers are completed, the next step is to orient all yellow pieces to face up, without worrying about their exact positions.

OLL Solving Methods

  • Full OLL: Uses 57 algorithms to complete this step in one move.
  • 2-Look OLL: A beginner-friendly method using only 3-4 algorithms
  • A trained speedcuber can solve OLL in one fast algorithm (2-3 seconds).
