PLL (Permutation of the Last Layer)

The final step in CFOP is correctly positioning all last-layer pieces. After completing OLL, the top face is entirely yellow, but the edge and corner pieces still need to be arranged in their correct positions.

PLL Solving Methods

  • Full PLL: Uses 21 algorithms to solve this step in one move.
  • 2-Look PLL: A beginner-friendly method using only 6 algorithms
  • A skilled speedcuber can complete PLL in under 2 seconds.
